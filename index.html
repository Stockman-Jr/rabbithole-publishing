Rabbithole

Rabbithole is an indie videogame publishing company run by a small group of people with a great passion for videogames. From professional gamers to veterans of the gaming industry, we have many years of experience in both playing videogames and creating them. Now we're looking for the next wave of game developers to take the stage.

About us

We're excited to find the next wave of talented indie game developers with original, fun ideas and we are here to help your vision come to life. We've had our success in the industry, and now we want to see you succeed! We're looking for:

  • Innovation
  • Passion & Artistry
  • Devs with a strong, clear vision
  • Devs with deep gaming experience and understanding

Check out our Games Section below to see our partners and what they've created, or what's in development!

What we do

We can offer developer-friendly contracts where developers can maintain 100% creative control. We're here to offer support and knowledge if needed, but we can also provide developers with any publishing needs they might have, which could be:

  • Funding
  • Marketing
  • Development Support
  • Porting & Localization

If you need a partner that can help your game stand out in a crowded space like the gaming industry, we're here to help! Click the button down below to go to our application page!
